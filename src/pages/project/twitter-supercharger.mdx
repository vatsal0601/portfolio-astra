## What is Twitter Supercharger?

It's a chrome extension that allows users to add Markdown formatting to their tweets. This easy-to-use extension provides users with a more versatile and streamlined method of composing and sharing their thoughts on Twitter.

## Tech-Stack

- MarkedJS
- HighlightJS
